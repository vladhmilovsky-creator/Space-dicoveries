Ongoing Space Missions

Perseverance (Mars 2020)

The Perseverance rover continues to explore Jezero Crater, searching for signs of ancient life and collecting rock samples for future return to Earth.

James Webb Space Telescope (JWST)

JWST is revolutionizing astronomy by peering deep into the early universe and capturing galaxies that formed only a few hundred million years after the Big Bang.

Lucy Mission

Lucy is NASA’s first mission to explore the Trojan asteroids—ancient remnants of the early solar system orbiting near Jupiter.
